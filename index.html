About MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni

MS Dhoni, often referred to as "Captain Cool," is a former Indian international cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen and captains in the history of cricket.

Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Dhoni made his international debut in 2004 and quickly rose through the ranks with his exceptional wicket-keeping skills and powerful batting.
