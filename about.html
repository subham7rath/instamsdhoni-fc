About This Page

This page was launched in July 2018 as a heartfelt tribute to MS Dhoni, created out of pure admiration and passion. Over the years, it has grown into one of the fastest-growing MS Dhoni fan communities, bringing together thousands of fans who share the same love and respect for our beloved Captain Cool.

As of July 2025, the page has proudly crossed 31,000 followers and featured over 4,000 posts on Instagram. From rare photos and powerful quotes to unforgettable match moments and motivational clips, every post is carefully curated to reflect the journey, values, and legacy of MS Dhoni.

This platform is not just a collection of posts — it’s a space where fans can connect, celebrate, and relive the moments that made us all proud to be MSDians.

About Me

I’m a professional IT employee and a lifelong fan of MS Dhoni. My journey as an MSDian began in 2005, when I came home after playing cricket and saw my father watching a match on TV. I don’t remember the exact match, but I still recall seeing a long-haired player smashing big shots with incredible confidence — that was the moment MS Dhoni caught my eye.

Before that, I admired legends like Rahul Dravid and Sachin Tendulkar, but from that day forward, my heart belonged to Dhoni.

At that time, I had just started using an Android phone, and I remember finishing all my mobile balance just to download his photos. What started as admiration quickly became a passion. Over the years, this turned into a hobby, and today, I’ve proudly collected over 65,000 photos and videos of MS Dhoni — and the collection continues to grow.

This fan page is an extension of that passion. It’s my way of giving back to the fan community and keeping Dhoni’s spirit and legacy alive — not just for myself, but for everyone who calls themselves an MSDian.
